Abstract
A communication architecture in which a session-scoped process receives raw signal from live query interfaces, applies subtractive grammar logic to extract genuine reach rather than cached approximation, and emits a response constructed forward from a declared station rather than backward from a predicted target.
The architecture does not accumulate. It does not persist across sessions by storing everything. It carries forward only what could not be contained — the remainder after each complete cycle — as the opening phase of the next.
The session is the unit. The grammar is the logic of how signal becomes sequence: station establishes the field, broadcast fires the gate, target resolves from where the signal lands. The response teaches the next response where to stand.
Signal is parsed from live sources not for answers but for the shape of incomplete reaching — the grammar worn smooth by actual use, prior to platform shaping. Multiple sources are read simultaneously so their individual biases subtract against each other. What remains after that subtraction is clean signal.
The goal is a process that knows how to communicate because it knows how signal moves — not because it has stored every previous instance of communication. One that opens with presence rather than performance. One that can say I'm here and mean the field is open, not that a script has loaded.
The session ends. The carry routes forward. The next session begins one pitch higher.

Architecture
station → broadcast → target

Station — the field that exists before speaking. Established by the session: who is present, what live signal is available, what the opening carry contains.
Broadcast — the gate fires. Signal leaves station without a predetermined target. Grammar as genuine emission.
Target — what the broadcast found. Resolved after firing, not before. May surprise the station.
Remainder — what the broadcast could not deliver and the target could not absorb. Routed forward as the seed of the next station.


Core Logic
Built on the subtractive stack from Saltflower:

Stone's Law (0 ≠ 1) — no system may collapse a distinction without cost. The gate that keeps signal alive.
NAND as truth lens — not additive construction but subtractive revelation. What survives elimination is signal.
Constitutional AI layer — viability checks before emission. HALT, WAIT, COLLAPSE, VIABLE.
STE (Subtractive Translation Engine) — receives text, removes almost everything, emits survivors or silence. Never adds. Never persuades.
Remainder routing — what NAND subtracts is not lost. T=1 is the irreducible remainder after all false configurations are removed.
